About us

Our old lives may be on pause but for many, our minds are busier than ever,

overwhelmed by the uncertainty and unease this pandemic has brought.

Whether you need cooking inspiration, to discover a new route to walk,

or some soothing playlists and podcasts to listen to, we can guide you in the right direction.


Our aim is to provide uplifting moments of light relief, through a curation of our

favourite ideas and activities to fill your days with a little positivity,

whether you need to escape, create or to be inspired.
